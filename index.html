Serendipity Place

“Serendipity Place” is a newly built, self-contained one bedroom annex available to rent in the picturesque village of Great Shelford, only 4 miles South of Cambridge.

The accommodation comprises of one large double bedroom, bathroom with walk in shower, living room with double sofa bed and fully equipped kitchen area. There is a flat screen TV with free view channels in the living area and Wi-Fi is available in all rooms. Washing facilities include washing machine and iron with ironing board.

The annex is attached to the main house but has an independent entrance and there is parking available on the driveway.

Serendipity Place is located in a quiet conservation area within walking distance to all the local amenities in the village (for more information on what’s available in the village: shelford.lucidata.com).
