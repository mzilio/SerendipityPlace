“Woodlands lodge” is a newly built self-contained one bedroom annex available to rent in the picturesque village of Great Shelford, only 4 miles South of Cambridge.

The accommodation comprises of one large double bedroom, bathroom with walk in shower and living room with fully equipped kitchen area. There is a TV in the living area, Wi-Fi is available in all rooms and iron with ironing board are also provided. Washing facilities are also available on minimum staying of 1 week.

The annex is attached to the main house but has an independent entrance and there is parking available on the driveway off the main road. The actual house is located in a quiet conservation area within walking distance to all the local amenities in the village (for more information on what’s available in the village: http://shelford.lucidata.com/directory.htm).
